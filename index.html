Privacy Policy for TYR

Last updated: December 2025

TYR respects your privacy. TYR is designed as an offline-first fitness application and does not sell or share your personal data.

Information We Collect

TYR stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Workout and exercise logs
  • Training routines and program data
  • Body measurements and fitness statistics (if entered by you)

Optional Health Integrations

If you enable Apple Health (iOS) or Health Connect (Android), TYR may read the following data to provide analysis and insights:

  • Steps
  • Sleep
  • Heart rate
  • Heart rate variability (HRV)
  • Body weight

This data is stored locally on your device and is not sold or shared.

How Your Data Is Used

  • Display workout history
  • Analyze training progress
  • Generate charts and insights

Data Sharing

TYR does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. Optional cloud sync features may be added in the future. If implemented, this policy will be updated.

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by uninstalling the app or using in-app reset options where available.

Children's Privacy

TYR is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.
